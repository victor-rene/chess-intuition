The World Chess Championship 2014 was a match between the world champion Magnus
Carlsen and challenger Viswanathan Anand, to determine the World Chess Champion.
It was held from 7 to 28 November 2014, under the auspices of the World Chess
Federation (FIDE) in Sochi, Russia.

The match was decided after eleven of twelve scheduled games. On 23 November
2014 Carlsen retained his title, winning three games, losing one and drawing
seven.
