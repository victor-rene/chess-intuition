In the game selection screen, select an event from the list on the left.
The players will shown. Click on a player picture to display biographic
information. On the right panel, select a game and click the load button
at the bottom.

During a game you can click the switch button to alternate between learn
mode and play mode. In learning mode, you follow the moves and try to
remember them. In the playing mode, you replay those moves as accurately
as possible to get the best score. Scores are ranked from S to E.
